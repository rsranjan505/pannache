Palm Jumeirah

Location Dubai
Type Residential
Year 2022

The Palm Jumeirah Project represents the epitome of coastal serenity.

Upon entry, guests are immediately swept away into an enchanting interior where the boundaries between indoors and outdoors fade into insignificance. The interplay of natural light and meticulously placed coastal-inspired elements create an ethereal space, where time slows down and the worries of the world melt away.

The sand-toned palette is harmonious, selected to recall sun-kissed beaches and soft, powdery shores. Each carefully selected accent is inspired by the vastness of the ocean, breathing life into the space and creating a sanctuary that offers an escape from the pace of everyday living. Each of the senses is captivated by the charm of hand-embroidered wallpaper, delicately adorned with patterns reminiscent of ocean waves and marine life.

The bedrooms have also been designed with a sea-scape inspiration. Here, the gentle breeze whispers through billowing curtains, carrying with it the faint scent of saltwater, setting the scene for utmost tranquility and relaxation.
